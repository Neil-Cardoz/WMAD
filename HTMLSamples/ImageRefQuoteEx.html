According to wikipedia, ISRO's main objectives for the Chandrayaan - 3 mission were:

  1. Getting the lander to land softly on the moon's surface.
  2. Observing and demonstrating the lander's driving capability on the Moon.
  3. Conducting experiments to understand material on the lunar surface.
